Genetic Diversity Lab

For this experiment, we took samples of cheek cells to seperate our DNA with DNA extraction, then we expanded a specific section of our DNA, called the D1S80 VNTR, into billions of copies with PCR amplification, then we compared our DNA to our classmates using the length of the strands through gel electrophoresis to discover the human diversity in our classroom. Before we started the experiment, we conducted an interview within our table group to make predictions on what results we would get.
